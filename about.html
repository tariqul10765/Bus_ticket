Who We Are ?

Safetravel is a super website that aims to simplify and resolve day to day needs while making life easier and efficient. Safetravel wants to take care of your daily need so you can use your time in fun-filled ad joyous occasions.

Safetravel started its journey in 2020 with the intent to make life easy for the masses of Bangladesh. It began by addressing the most challenging problems Bangladeshis faced in their day to day life by digitizing the online ticket booking process. Safetravel is an innovative technology platform that intends to empower people by solving & simplifying their daily needs problem. Safetravel is on its way to become the trusted one stop solution for users enabling them to focus on enjoying their lives hassle-free.
